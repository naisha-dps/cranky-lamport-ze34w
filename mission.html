OUR MISSION


Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. Tesla was founded in 2003 by a group of engineers who wanted to prove that people didn't need to compromise to drive electric – that electric vehicles can be better, quicker and more fun to drive than gasoline cars.
Tesla, Inc’s mission and vision statements both share the company’s organizational objective of leading the world to sustainable energy consumption. A group of engineers founded Tesla in 2003 with the sole purpose of proving that we don’t have to sacrifice any benefits when we choose electric cars over conventionally fueled cars. Today, in 2021, it would be an understatement to say that electric cars are just as good as conventionally fueled cars, if not better. All thanks to Tesla, Inc. for moving forward with its goal.


Tesla, Inc’s mission statement is,
“to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy.”


OUR CORE VALUE


Tesla does not have any fixed core values.
The company deeply cares about sustainable energy, innovation, manufacturing quality cars.
